My First week at AkiraChix

By Kabonge

Good things i have so far experienced;

  1. Very delicious food.
  2. The instructors make sure you understand what is taught,they are very helpful.
  3. The place is a very great study environment.
  4. The help of alumni who are very supportive and encourage us always.

The not so good things so far;

  • The weather especially in the morning.
